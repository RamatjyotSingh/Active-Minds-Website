Welcome to UM Active Minds

We are a chapter of Active Minds Inc., a dedicated student-led organization at the University of Manitoba focused on promoting mental health awareness. Through educational initiatives, engaging events, and strong advocacy, we strive to empower our campus community. Join us in our mission to educate, raise awareness, and advocate for mental health throughout the University of Manitoba and beyond. Together, we can foster meaningful conversations and create a lasting impact on mental well-being.
